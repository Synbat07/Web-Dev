Pasta Recipe

  • Time: 8–10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Salt 1 tablespoon
Butter or sauce 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Pour water into a pot and bring it to a boil.
Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of salt.
Step 3: Add the pasta and cook for 8–10 minutes.
Step 4: Drain the water using a colander.
Step 5: Add 1 tablespoon of butter or sauce and mix.

Salad Recipe

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 100 g
Tomato 1
Dressing 1 tablespoon

Mix all ingredients in a bowl and serve fresh.
